## 5. Platform Services

### 5.1. Task Posting

- Requesters may post tasks with descriptions, rewards, and deadlines
- Rewards must be funded to the escrow smart contract
- Once funded, tasks become available for workers to claim
- Requesters may cancel unfunded tasks at any time
- Funded tasks may only be cancelled if unclaimed (subject to smart contract rules)

### 5.2. Task Claiming and Completion

- Workers may claim funded tasks on a first-come-first-served basis
- Workers must submit work before the deadline
- Submitted work is reviewed by the Requester
- Upon approval, payment is automatically released from escrow

### 5.3. Disputes

- Either party may raise a dispute on submitted work
- Disputes are currently resolved manually by Platform administrators
- A 5% dispute fee applies to disputed tasks
- Dispute resolution decisions are final
